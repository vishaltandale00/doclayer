guide · scenario 02

Two-up planning — divergent shells over a shared outline.

0–5s · two-up planning

Two panes, same outline tree. Vishal’s pane shows the evidence panel scraped from shared links; Akhil’s pane shows a tension tracker. Different views over identical state.

5–10s · divergent shells

Both cursors land in the outline. A section is reordered; both views update with a graph delta, not a bulk re-render. The harnesses diverged on layout but never on data.

10–16s · shared outline reorder

Akhil disagrees on a sub-heading. The tension tracker opens a row. Resolution flows through chat inside the tension card itself — not a side channel, part of the grammar.

16–22s · tension resolution

Tension resolves. The outline locks the section. Phase chip on the lifecycle ribbon advances toward “draft”.
