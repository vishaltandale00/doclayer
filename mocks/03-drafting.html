Meta-harnesses

A series, target: AI engineers and builders.

3. Building meta-harnesses

If the harness is the controller, the meta-harness is the controller that authors controllers. It evolves the harness as the task and the user evolve. Two open questions follow: how do you express the search space, and how do you stay within the user's prior?

The search space isn't free; every harness atom is typed and every mutation is a delta against a manifest the Architect has to emit, which means the model is composing inside a grammar instead of producing raw markup it might or might not get right. That's not the same problem as the one most UI-generating models are trying to solve, and the framing change matters more than the architectural one — once you're picking atoms out of a kit the failure modes look like under-coverage of the kit, not hallucination.

guide · scenario 03

Typing live, agents fire inline, the harness cycles intent state.

0–6s · typing live

The cursor types into the editor. The harness-strip pip reads “composing”. The right rail is quiet — the writer is in flow.

6–10s · AI agents fire inline

Research and adversarial agents augment the prose in place: citation badges attach to claims; a contradiction nudge flags a hedge. Augments are atoms, not popups.

10–16s · harness intent state cycles

The cursor stalls. The harness shifts intent state from “compose” to “revise”. The right rail expands; three revision variants surface for the stalled paragraph.

16–20s · stall → variant → accept

A variant is accepted. The paragraph swaps in place. The voice sampler updates Akhil’s density meter to reflect the new tightness.

20–22s · voice fidelity nudge

Density has drifted toward Vishal’s voice. A tiny chip nudges to tighten; one click accepts. The lesson commits to memory.

what this provesThe harness has intent state. Same text, different surface for write vs. revise — and it switches without a mode button.
